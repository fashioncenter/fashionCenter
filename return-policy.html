Return Policy

Overview

At M. Fashion, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our return policy is designed to make the return process as smooth and convenient as possible.

Return Window

30-Day Return Period

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Processing Time

Returns are typically processed within 5-7 business days after receipt.

Refund Timeline

Refunds are issued within 3-5 business days after return approval.

Return Conditions

Eligible Items

  • Unused items in original packaging
  • Items with original tags attached
  • Items in original condition
  • Items with proof of purchase

Non-Eligible Items

  • Used or worn items
  • Items without original packaging
  • Items without original tags
  • Items purchased on sale or clearance

Return Process

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and select the item(s) you wish to return.

2

Print Label

Print the return shipping label provided in your account.

3

Package Items

Pack the items securely with all original packaging and tags.

4

Ship Back

Drop off the package at your nearest shipping location.

Refund Information

Original Payment Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for purchase.

Store Credit

You may choose to receive store credit instead of a refund.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item was defective.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for different sizes, colors, or styles of the same item. To process an exchange:

  1. Initiate a return through your account
  2. Select "Exchange" as the return reason
  3. Choose the new item you'd like to receive
  4. Follow the return shipping instructions

Exchanges are subject to availability. If the desired item is out of stock, we'll process a refund instead.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about our return policy or need assistance with a return, please contact our customer support team.
